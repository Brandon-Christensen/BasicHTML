Hiking


Unlike sports, when I was a kid I hated hiking. I hated it
when my parents would drag me on a hike. As I get older and older,
my appreciation for hiking has gone up. I really started enjoying hiking after
I went on a night hike to Delicate Arch. Now I try to go hiking when I can.
